Advanced Layout and Style

  1. Space between boxes - add space between the content (text and image) and the box border

    2. Let's at what we have so far. The hyperlinks inside the leftbox are too close to the borders of the box. We need to add some space between the box border and the hyperlinks. Click the to hide the image and proceed.

The model shows that the padding is what we need to achieve the space between the box border and the content. Just as margin, padding also has four sides: left, right, top and buttom. After some experimenting, I found the following works best for me:
padding-top: 40px;
padding-left: 10px;
padding-buttom: 20px;
padding-right: 10px;
